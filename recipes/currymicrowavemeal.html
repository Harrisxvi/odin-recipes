Curry Microwave Meal

Description

A pre-made meal for lazy louts, that you can just whack in the microwave.

Ingredients

  • Hearty Food Co. Microwave Meal

Steps

  1. Pierce film with a fork
  2. Whack it in the microwave for 7 mins at full power
  3. Peel back film & stir contents.
  4. Back in the microwave for 2 minutes
  5. Leave to stand for 2 minutes
