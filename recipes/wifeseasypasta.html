Hannah Browns Easy Pasta Recipe

Description

My wifes easy pasta recipe. Helpful when it's a late night home from work!

Ingrdients

  • 3 handfuls of Penne Pasta
  • 1 Block of Halloumi Cheese
  • 1 Bowl of Grated Cheese
  • 1 Dolmio Stir in Pot

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta for 10minutes.
  2. Drain & cut the halloumi before frying it until gold & brown.
  3. Drain the pasta & add the Dolmio Stir in pot.
  4. Add the fried halloumi to the pasta and stir together.
  5. Leave to stand for 2 minutes before stirring.
